Shrine Helper

Quickly find any missing shrines!

A Few Notes

  • This tool won't work if you don't have all the towers activated.
  • If you have it, the Travel Medallion must be moved out of the way of any of the potential shrine locations.
    I recommend placing it in the middle of Hyrule field east of the Central Tower.
  • And of course make sure your shrine tracker is turned on.

Alright that's it. Happy hunting!

Instructions

  1. Upload screenshots

    Open up your map and move the cursor near position that's shown in each example. Take the screenshots and upload them.




  2. Show hints

    Choose if the tool should show hints for shrines instead of exact locations.
    Show Hints (recommended)
    Show Exact Locations

  3. Find Shrines!
